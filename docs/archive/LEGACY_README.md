## 🎓 AI AGENT CAPABILITIES

### 1. Idea Generation
- **Input**: Topic/niche, target publications
- **Output**: 10+ ideas with:
  - Publication-ready headlines
  - Unique angles
  - Timely hooks (news-pegged when possible)
  - Data points to include
  - Expert sources to contact
  - Acceptance score (1-10)
  - Revenue estimate ($500-$2,000)

### 2. Outline Creation
- **Input**: Article idea, target publication
- **Output**: Detailed outline with:
  - Opening hook (100-150 words)
  - 3-5 main sections with bullet points
  - Required data and sources
  - Expert quote placeholders
  - SEO keywords
  - Smooth transitions

### 3. Article Draft Writing
- **Input**: Outline, publication style
- **Output**: Full article featuring:
  - Publication-specific tone and reading level
  - Proper citations [Source, Year]
  - Expert quotes [Name, Title, Company]
  - Subheadings every 200-250 words
  - Actionable conclusion
  - Target word count (700-1,800 depending on publication)

### 4. Pitch Generation
- **Input**: Article idea, editor name
- **Output**: Compelling pitch email with:
  - Subject line
  - Hook (first 2 sentences)
  - Why now (timely relevance)
  - Why me (your expertise)
  - What you'll deliver
  - Professional closing
  - 150-200 words (concise!)

### 5. Research Automation
- **Input**: Topic keywords
- **Output**: Research report including:
  - Recent news articles (last 7 days)
  - Key trends identified
  - Data points and statistics
  - Content gaps (opportunities!)
  - Recommended angles for pitching

### 6. Acceptance Scoring
- **Input**: Article idea, target publication
- **Output**: Detailed evaluation:
  - Overall score (1-10)
  - Timeliness score
  - Originality score
  - Relevance score
  - Data potential score
  - Expert access score
  - Strengths (2-3 bullets)
  - Weaknesses (2-3 bullets)
  - Improvement suggestions
  - Estimated payment range
